# CEO Audience Search Queries: What Non-Technical Leaders Actually Google

**Research date:** 2026-04-17
**Method:** Web search analysis across Reddit, Quora, SEO data, YouTube suggestions, Google autocomplete patterns, and AI agency landing page keyword research.

**Key insight:** Business leaders do not search for "AI agents," "workflow automation frameworks," or "Claude Code skills." They search in pain language: the task they hate doing, the employee they cannot afford, the hours they want back. The word "AI" appears in their searches only as a modifier on a business problem they already understand.

---

## Search Volume Context

- "Business automation" is an evergreen high-volume term (Google Keyword Planner estimates 10K-100K monthly searches globally for core variants)
- "AI tools for business" surged 3x from 2023-2025 per Google Trends
- "AI automation agency" is a fast-growing term with 62% YoY competition increase in 2025
- Long-tail queries ("automate my invoicing with AI") are 60% more likely to trigger AI Overviews, meaning they attract serious searchers
- 68% of small businesses now use AI tools regularly, but most search for solutions to specific problems, not "AI" generically

## CONTENT STRATEGY IMPLICATIONS FOR CLAUDEFLOWS

### Highest-opportunity query themes (volume x intent x fit):
1. **"how to automate [specific task] without coding"** -- direct match to skill-based automation offering
2. **"best AI tools for small business 2026"** -- listicle/comparison content that positions ClaudeFlows
3. **"AI automation for [industry]"** -- niche landing pages for verticals
4. **"how much does AI automation cost"** -- pricing transparency builds trust with buyers
5. **"hire AI consultant vs DIY"** -- comparison content that frames consulting-through-ClaudeFlows

### Words that appear in their vocabulary but NOT in ours:
- "set up" (not "implement" or "deploy")
- "someone to help me" (not "consulting engagement")
- "save time" (not "increase operational efficiency")
- "without coding" / "no code" (not "low-code platform")
- "for my business" (not "for SMBs" or "for enterprises")
- "how much does it cost" (not "pricing tiers")
- "get started" (not "onboard")

### Content formats that match these queries:
- "How I automated X in my [industry] business" (case study blog posts)
- "5 things you can automate today without coding" (listicle)
- "AI automation cost calculator" (interactive tool)
- "What an AI automation consultant actually does" (education/trust content)
- YouTube: "I automated my entire [task] with AI -- here's how" (tutorial)
