# Google Ads Strategy: AI Automation Consulting for Business Leaders

*Research date: April 17, 2026*

---

## 1. Market Landscape: What Shows Up Today

The AI automation consulting ad space is dominated by SaaS platforms (Zapier, Make, UiPath) and large agencies, not solo consultants. This is actually an opportunity -- most ads speak "tool language" not "business outcome language." A consultant selling transformation (not software) occupies a different mental slot.

**Who's advertising on AI automation queries:**
- SaaS automation platforms (Zapier, Make, Monday.com, UiPath)
- Large consulting firms (Accenture, Deloitte digital transformation)
- AI tool aggregators and comparison sites
- Managed service agencies ("we'll run your AI")
- Very few solo consultants or small studios

**Gap:** Almost nobody speaks directly to a non-technical business owner in plain language. The ads are either enterprise-speak or tool-speak. There's room for "I'll personally automate your business" positioning.

## 2. CPC Estimates by Keyword Category

Based on 2025-2026 industry data. B2B services average CPC is $2.96 cross-industry (Q1 2026), but AI/automation keywords run higher due to competition from funded SaaS companies.

| Keyword Category | Example Keywords | Est. CPC Range | Competition |
|---|---|---|---|
| High-intent service | "hire AI consultant", "AI automation agency" | $8-18 | High |
| Problem-aware | "automate my business", "too much manual work" | $3-8 | Medium |
| Solution-aware | "business process automation", "workflow automation" | $6-15 | High (SaaS competition) |
| Industry-specific | "automate accounting firm", "AI for real estate agency" | $3-7 | Low-Medium |
| Pain-point | "spending too much time on admin", "business efficiency" | $2-5 | Medium |
| Long-tail | "how to use AI to save time in my business" | $1-4 | Low |

**Key insight:** Generic "AI automation" keywords are expensive ($10-18) because SaaS companies with massive budgets bid on them. Industry-specific and pain-point keywords are 3-5x cheaper and convert better for consulting services.

**CPC trend:** 12% year-over-year increase across all industries. Budget accordingly -- what costs $5 today costs $5.60 next year.

## 6. Budget Strategy: $20/Day ($600/Month)

### Recommended Split

| Campaign | Daily Budget | Monthly | Keywords | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Bottom-Funnel (Service) | $10/day | $300 | Group A keywords | Direct leads |
| Problem-Aware (Pain) | $7/day | $210 | Group B keywords | Warm leads + remarketing |
| Industry-Specific | $3/day | $90 | Group C + industry terms | List building + remarketing |

### Month 1: Learning Phase
- Start with bottom-funnel keywords ONLY ($20/day, all on Group A)
- Manual CPC bidding, set max CPC at $12
- Goal: gather conversion data (need 15-30 conversions before switching to automated bidding)
- Review search term reports DAILY for the first 2 weeks to add negative keywords

### Month 2: Expand
- Switch bottom-funnel campaign to Target CPA bidding (if enough conversion data)
- Add Group B problem-aware campaign at $7/day
- Start building remarketing audiences from website visitors

### Month 3: Optimize + Scale
- Add industry-specific campaigns for your best-converting verticals
- Enable remarketing campaign ($3/day) targeting people who visited but didn't convert
- Kill underperforming keywords, double down on winners

### Budget Protection Rules
- Never spend more than $12 CPC (set as max bid)
- Pause any keyword with 50+ clicks and 0 conversions
- Review search terms weekly, add negatives aggressively
- Set ad schedule to business hours + evening (Mon-Fri 7am-9pm, Sat 9am-2pm)
- Exclude ages 18-24 (students/job seekers, not buyers)

## 8. Landing Page Recommendations

### Structure (Single Page, Top to Bottom)

1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline: "I Automate the Busywork Killing Your Business"
   - Subhead: "Custom AI automations built for your exact workflows. Save 10-20 hours per week. No technical skills required."
   - CTA button: "Get Your Free Workflow Audit"
   - Trust bar: client logos or "Trusted by 20+ business owners"

2. **Problem Section**
   - "Sound familiar?" with 3-4 pain points (copy-pasting data, manual follow-ups, drowning in admin)
   - Each with a "before/after" AI automation contrast

3. **How It Works (3 Steps)**
   - Step 1: Free Audit (I analyze your workflows)
   - Step 2: Custom Build (I build your automations)
   - Step 3: Launch + Train (You start saving time)

4. **Social Proof**
   - 2-3 client testimonials with specific results ("Saved 12 hours/week", "ROI in first month")
   - Video testimonial if available (pages with video convert up to 86% higher)

5. **What I Automate (Examples)**
   - Client onboarding, invoicing, scheduling, email follow-ups, reporting, data entry
   - Industry-specific examples if running industry campaigns

6. **Pricing Transparency**
   - "Projects start at $500" or "Flat-rate packages from $X"
   - Removes price anxiety, filters out non-serious inquiries

7. **FAQ Section**
   - "Do I need technical skills?" (No)
   - "How long does it take?" (Most projects: 1-2 weeks)
   - "What tools do you work with?" (Your existing tools)
   - "What if it doesn't work?" (Guarantee)

8. **Final CTA**
   - Repeat the primary offer: "Book Your Free 30-Minute Strategy Call"
   - Simple form: Name, Email, Business Type, Biggest Time Waster (dropdown)

### Technical Requirements
- Load time under 3 seconds (mobile and desktop)
- Mobile-first design (50%+ of B2B searches now mobile)
- No navigation menu (keep them on the page)
- Form above the fold OR sticky CTA button
- Thank-you page with calendar booking link (Calendly or Cal.com)
- Google Ads conversion tracking pixel on thank-you page
- Facebook pixel for cross-platform remarketing

### Build separate landing pages for:
- Each industry vertical you target
- "Free audit" offer vs "book a call" offer
- Different pain points (A/B test)

## 9. Platform Comparison: Where to Spend $600/Month

| Factor | Google Ads | LinkedIn Ads | Meta (FB/IG) Ads |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CPC** | $4-12 (B2B services) | $5-15 | $1-4 |
| **Lead quality** | High (active searchers) | Highest (job title targeting) | Lower (interruptive) |
| **Min viable budget** | $500/mo | $1,500/mo+ | $300/mo |
| **Targeting precision** | Intent-based (keywords) | Role-based (job title, company size) | Interest + behavior |
| **Best for** | Capturing existing demand | Creating demand among decision-makers | Remarketing + awareness |
| **Learning curve** | Medium | High | Medium |
| **Time to results** | 2-4 weeks | 4-8 weeks | 1-2 weeks |
| **ROAS for consulting** | 3-7x (mature) | 1.8x avg (2025 data) | Variable |

### Verdict for $600/Month Budget

**Start with Google Ads only.** Here's why:

1. **LinkedIn is too expensive at $600/month.** You need $1,500+/month to gather enough data for optimization. At $600, you'd get 40-80 clicks and maybe 1-2 leads -- not enough signal.

2. **Google captures existing demand.** People searching "hire AI consultant" already want what you sell. LinkedIn and Meta interrupt people who weren't looking. At low budgets, capturing demand beats creating it.

3. **Meta is best as a supplement, not primary.** Use it for $3-5/day remarketing once you have Google Ads traffic flowing. Retarget website visitors with case study content.

### If Budget Grows to $1,500+/Month
Split: Google Ads 60% ($900) + LinkedIn 25% ($375) + Meta remarketing 15% ($225)
