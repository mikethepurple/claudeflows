# Voice DNA & Brand Guide

Extract your brand voice into a guide anyone can follow

## What it does

Your brand sounds different in every email, social post, and landing page because there is no written guide. Paste 5-10 samples of your best content and get back a complete brand voice guide with tone dimensions, vocabulary rules, do's and don'ts, and before/after rewrites showing your voice applied consistently.

## Install

```bash
claudeflows install brand-voice
```

## Usage

```
claude
> /extract-voice
```

Paste 5-10 content samples or provide file paths. Optionally describe your audience and any focus areas. More samples and mixed content types (blog, email, social) produce a richer guide.

## What you get

- Voice summary distilling your brand identity in 2-3 sentences
- Tone dimensions scored on 4-5 spectrums with evidence
- Vocabulary guide with words to use and avoid
- Do's and don'ts (5-7 rules per column)
- Before/after rewrites showing your voice applied
- Platform-specific adaptations for email, social, docs, and landing pages

## Requirements

- Claude Code

## Example

> /extract-voice
> [pastes 6 blog posts and 2 marketing emails] Audience: B2B SaaS founders, seed to Series A

Analyzes your samples, identifies a voice that is "direct, technically credible, occasionally irreverent," maps it on spectrums (formal-casual: 3.5/5, technical-accessible: 4/5), lists vocabulary patterns ("ship" over "deploy," never "synergy"), and rewrites three generic sentences in your voice to demonstrate the guide in action.
