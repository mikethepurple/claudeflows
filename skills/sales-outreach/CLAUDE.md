# Sales Outreach Sequence Generator

This skill generates production-ready cold outreach email sequences.

## Usage

Run the `generate-sequence` skill. It will ask for your target persona, value proposition, and preferred tone, then produce a complete 5-7 step email sequence with A/B subject line variants, personalization variables, and send timing.

## Output

The output is a single copy-paste-ready document with each email step clearly labeled. Personalization variables use `{{variable}}` syntax compatible with all major outreach tools (Instantly, Lemlist, Apollo, Smartlead, etc.).

## Design Principles

- Every email earns the right to send the next one
- Subject lines optimize for curiosity, not clickbait
- Bodies are short enough to read on a phone
- Each email has exactly one CTA
- The sequence ends with a graceful breakup email that often gets the highest reply rate
